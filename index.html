Alternative Payment Methods

Alternative Currency: BG Coin

What is it?

BG Coin is a compensation currency for our platform Bargreen used while barter.

Is it useful?

Yes! BG Coin helps you to compensate other users while bartering, in case the value of the products is not satisfiable for any one end.

Any use in the long run?

Ofcourse! We have planned to launch BG Coin after a year of the product's launch in the form of a cryptocurrency, which will promise huge returns.

Where can I get it?

The BG Coin can be purchased in the in-app store for superb prices.

Can I earn it without spending money?

Yes! When you barter, you have a chance to earn BG coins in the form of compensation. You can also earn them when you refer our app as per our referral program.

Is it redeemable in any way?

For Sure! The BG Coins can be redeemed for cool gift cards and vouchers in the store!
